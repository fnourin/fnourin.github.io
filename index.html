Research

My work focuses on spatial data science, GIS, and remote sensing for planning and environmental applications. I am particularly interested in how geospatial methods and survey-based models can help us understand risk, preparedness, water security, and urban change.

Urban Growth and Land Use / Land Cover Dynamics
Google Earth Engine · Land use / land cover · Urban sustainability
  • Analyzed land use/land cover categories and their sustainability implications using multi-temporal remote sensing imagery.
Water Security & REACH Project
Institute of Water and Flood Management (IWFM), BUET
  • Contributed to research on improving water security for vulnerable communities, including GIS-based land use mapping and change detection for Dhaka.
