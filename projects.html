A case study of selected Iowa cities for estimation of urban growth sustainability

Case Study · Urban Growth & Sustainability

Eighteen of twenty selected Iowa cities showed positive change in the Sustainability Index (SI) from 2011–2019, while Fort Dodge and Clinton declined (Clinton’s change was negligible). Fort Dodge showed a substantial drop linked to decreasing GDP, suggesting a need for policy-level economic intervention. This project recommends using SI to compare cities and prioritize planning and policy actions.
